How it works
📤

1. Upload X-Ray

Drag and drop or browse for a posteroanterior (PA) chest X-ray in JPEG or PNG format. The image is processed locally and sent securely over HTTPS.

🧠

2. DenseNet-121 Inference

The image is resized to 224×224, normalised, and passed through 121 densely-connected convolutional layers pretrained on ImageNet, fine-tuned on 4,200+ TB radiographs.

📊

3. Focal-Loss Classification

A sigmoid head outputs a TB probability. Focal loss (γ=2, α=0.75) down-weights easy normal cases and aggressively penalises missed TB — critical for an imbalanced medical dataset.

🩺

4. Screening result

You receive a label (Normal / Tuberculosis), confidence score, and probability. A disclaimer reminds users to confirm findings with a qualified radiologist.
